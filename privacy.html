Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how MacFlyToTheWorld Scheduler collects, uses, and protects user data when accessing and interacting with our automation services.

Information We Collect

We only process the data necessary to authenticate with TikTok and publish user-approved content. No personal data is stored on external servers. All processing occurs locally within the user’s own automation platform (n8n).

How We Use Your Information

  • Authenticate your TikTok account through official API OAuth.
  • Upload videos or drafts according to your explicit automation workflow.
  • No data is sold, shared, or transmitted to third parties.

User Control

Users may revoke TikTok access tokens at any time through TikTok account settings.
